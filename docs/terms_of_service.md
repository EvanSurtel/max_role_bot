## 1. What This Is

Rank $ is a skill-based gaming platform on Discord. You can wager real money on Call of Duty Mobile matches against other players. The better player wins the pot. This is NOT gambling — outcomes are determined by player skill, not chance.

## 2. Who Can Use This

- You must be **18 years or older**.
- You must live in a location where skill-based gaming is legal (see Section 9 for restricted areas).
- One account per person. No alts, no sharing accounts.
- You must provide truthful information when you register.

## 3. How Deposits Work

You deposit money using **MoonPay**, a third-party payment processor. Here's how:

1. **Open your wallet** in the wallet channel and click **Deposit using Credit/Debit Card**
2. **Complete identity verification** with MoonPay (first time only — they need to verify your ID by law)
3. **Pay with your card**, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or bank transfer
4. **Funds arrive in your wallet** within a few minutes

**Things to know about deposits:**
- Minimum deposit: **$5**
- Deposits via Coinbase have **0% fee**. Other methods may charge 3-5%.
- Your bank or card company may also charge their own fees — that's between you and them
- Deposits are **non-refundable** once they hit your wallet
- You can also deposit directly from any wallet that supports USDC on the **Base** network

## 4. How Withdrawals Work

When you're ready to take your money out:

1. **Open your wallet** and click **Withdraw USDC**
2. **Enter the amount** and the wallet address you want to send it to
3. **Confirm** — your USDC is sent to that address on the Base network

From there, sell your USDC on an exchange (like Coinbase) and withdraw to your bank.

**Things to know about withdrawals:**
- Minimum withdrawal: **$5**
- No platform fee — the only cost is a tiny network gas fee (~$0.01-0.05)
- You must wager your deposit at least once before you can withdraw it
- Withdrawals are **final and cannot be reversed**
- You can withdraw to any wallet that supports USDC on the **Base** network

## 5. Your Wallet

When you register, the bot creates a wallet for you. This wallet holds your funds on the server. You can see your balance anytime by clicking **View My Wallet** in the wallet channel.

- **Available balance** — money you can use for wagers or withdraw
- **Held balance** — money locked in an active wager (returned when the match is decided)

Your wallet is secured with encryption. Only the bot can access it.

## 6. How Wagers Work

- Both players (or teams) put in the same entry amount
- The winner takes the full pot
- If captains disagree on who won, the match goes to dispute and staff resolves it
- If you don't show up within 15 minutes, you forfeit
- You can cancel a wager before someone accepts it, but not after
- All wager results are final once confirmed by both sides or resolved by staff
